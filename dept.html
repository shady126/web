WELCOME

The I.C.T Head of department, Dr Lawrence O Omotosho welcomes you to Osun State University and its prestigious ICT department.

I have the pleasure to welcome you to the website of Osun state University I.C.T department website, Nigeria. Our main campus is located in Osogbo, the capital of the State of Osun, where our ICT Department is situated. Licensed on 21 December, 2006, we opened our doors to full academic activities on 21 September 2007 and we have continued to make steady progress in the development of facilities and human resource to match the growing needs of the institution.

At Osun State University, we are driven by our vision and desire to be a centre of excellence providing high quality teaching and learning experience, which will engender the production of entrepreneurial graduates capable of impacting on their environment while being globally competitive. As an institution, we have committed ourselves to creating a unique institution committed to the pursuit of academic innovation, skill-based training and a tradition of excellence in teaching, research and community service..
